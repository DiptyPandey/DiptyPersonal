Poem For You

1 August 2024 ka tha woh pal khaas, Jab mili thi tu, meri duniya bani ek saath.
Tere saath sapne sajaye Disha naam ke pyaar se, Choti si pari hogi, hamesha tere yaar se..
Kumbha, Prayagraj, Vrindavan ki humne li planning, Kedarnath mein milegi pyaar ki divine blessing
Raaton ko ice cream khana, tera haath pakad ke chalna, Zindagi bhar tere saath har lamha muskurana.

Tujhe dunga kapde, ek guitar aur camera pyaara, Teri muskaan mein hi toh hai mera saara sahara.
Tu hi meri dua, tu hi har khushi ka reason hai, Janamdin pe bas kehna hai — tu meri life ka season hai.
